About Us

Growlrr Foods is a next-generation pet nutrition company built at the intersection of veterinary science, food engineering, and digital technology. We design rotation-based, human-grade meals that deliver safety in every pouch and resilience through variety.

Our patented nutritional architecture replaces static “complete formula” diets with a modular system of single-species, organ-forward meals fortified by precision premixes. Each pouch is retort-sterilized for stability and traceability, ensuring compliance with AAFCO and FEDIAF standards without a cold-chain.

Growlrr scales through OEM partnerships with certified export-grade manufacturers, reducing capital load while maintaining quality and regulatory control. Our digital platform personalizes feeding charts, manages subscriptions, and closes the feedback loop between pets, parents, and nutritionists.

Mission

To make scientifically precise, biologically appropriate food accessible to every pet parent — transforming feeding from a chore into a confident, data-guided act of care.
