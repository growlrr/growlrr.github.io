The Problem (Linear Trap)

Standard Kibbles over-fuel large breeds and underfeeds small breeds with high-starch calories spiking glycemic indices and causing chronic inflammation, insulin spike/crash and chronic metabolic syndrome.

The Solution (Metabolic Logic)

Growlrr utilizes calibrated allometry. By decoupling energy requirements from micronutrient density, we scale structural protein and fat strictly to metabolic mass—completely eliminating the need for inflammatory starches and synthetic fillers.

Obesity & Coupling

Feline metabolism is lifestyle-sensitive. Commercial diets physically couple fuel and nutrients; to reach baseline vitamins, indoor cats must over-consume energy, guaranteeing rapid obesity and insulin resistance.

The Solution (Decoupled Strategy)

Growlrr implements a Decoupled Strategy. We physically separate grocery fuel (kcal) from micronutrients (Blocks). You can safely restrict fuel independently without ever compromising your cat's absolute nutrient baseline.
