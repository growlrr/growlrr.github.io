A Science-First Companion Nutrition System

Precision Nutrition vs. Commercial Convenience.

The daily consumption of highly processed industrial food presents documented clinical risks. For companion animals, extruded kibble remains the norm despite heat-induced nutrient degradation and chronic dehydration.

Renal & Urinary Stress

Felines possess a low thirst drive. Extruded kibble forces a state of chronic low-grade dehydration, precipitating urolithiasis via high-concentration inorganic salts.

The Heat-Loss Deficit

High-heat retorting degrades thiamine (B1) and taurine. Synthetic spray-on vitamins suffer rapid oxidative degradation during shelf-life.

Decentralized Fresh Protein

Local procurement of fresh protein ensures high biological value, ~75% natural moisture intake, and absolute flavor without palatants.

Centralized NRC Blocks

Growlrr's CatCore™ and DogCore™ blocks bridge every nutritional gap using organic chelates and whole-food liver matrices.

Structure with Freedom

The core baseline is nutritionally absolute. The Flex Block system allows safe swaps for variety (Heart, Gizzard) while maintaining a strict Ca:P lock.
